About Us

Our Mission

At Grace Community Church, our mission is to spread the love of Christ and make disciples of all nations. We are committed to serving our community and sharing the gospel through our actions and words.

Our History

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for over three decades. We started as a small gathering in a local home and have grown into a vibrant congregation that serves hundreds of families.

Our Values

  • Faith: We believe in the power of faith to transform lives.
  • Community: We are dedicated to building a strong, supportive community.
  • Service: We are committed to serving others in the name of Christ.
  • Love: We strive to love everyone as Christ loves us.
