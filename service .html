Our Services

Join us for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. We offer a variety of services to meet the needs of our community.

Sunday Worship

Time: Every Sunday at 10:00 AM

Location: Main Sanctuary

Join us for a powerful time of worship, prayer, and teaching from the Word of God.

Bible Study

Time: Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM

Location: Fellowship Hall

Dive deeper into the Scriptures and grow in your faith with our weekly Bible study.

Youth Gathering

Time: Every Friday at 6:00 PM

Location: Youth Center

A dynamic and fun environment for teens to grow in their relationship with Christ.
